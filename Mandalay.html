Best places to visit in Mandalay

Mandalay, the second-largest city in Myanmar, is full of history and culture. Known for its royal heritage, the city features lively markets and traditional crafts. With its unique atmosphere and rich traditions, Mandalay is a must-visit destination in Myanmar.

Kuthodaw Pagoda

Kuthodaw Pagoda, known as the world’s largest book, features 729 marble slabs inscribed with Buddhist teachings, making it a significant cultural site in Mandalay.

U Bein Bridge

U Bein Bridge is the longest teak bridge in the world, crossing Taungthaman Lake. It’s a beautiful spot for sunset views and a taste of local life.

Mandalay Palace

Mandalay Palace is the last royal palace of Myanmar, surrounded by a large moat. It offers a glimpse into the country’s royal history and architecture.
