Best places to visit in Myanmar

Written by Allison Burgers, and Ladis Washerum

Located in Southeast Asia, Myanmar is a country full of rich traditions, beautiful landscapes, and historical sites. From the lively streets of Yangon to the cultural center of Mandalay and the peaceful mountain town of Kalaw, Myanmar offers something unique for every traveler. This guide explores these three amazing places, each showing a different side of the country—from impressive temples to scenic trails—making Myanmar a must-visit for those looking for adventure and culture.
