Best places to visit in Kalaw

Kalaw, a charming hill town in Myanmar, is known for its cool climate and scenic landscapes. Located in Shan State, it features historical buildings and a mix of cultures. Kalaw is popular for trekking to nearby villages and the famous Inle Lake. With its busy markets and quiet temples, itâ€™s a great place for nature lovers.

Shwe Oo Min Pagoda

Visit Shwe Oo Min Pagoda, a peaceful cave filled with beautiful Buddha statues and vibrant decorations in Kalaw.

Kalaw Town Market

Visit the lively Kalaw Town Market, where locals come to buy fresh fruits, vegetables, and handmade crafts. Experience the delicious flavors of Shan cuisine while enjoying the vibrant atmosphere and friendly vendors. It's a great place to immerse yourself in the local culture!

Kalaw Railway Station

Kalaw Railway Station, built during colonial times, is a charming historic landmark with beautiful views of the surrounding hills. This lovely stop invites travelers to take scenic journeys and enjoy the beauty of Myanmar's countryside.
